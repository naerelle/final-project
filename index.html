About

Radley Dekker is a specialised patisserie and experienced executive Chef. With over 40 year’s industry experience, he has mastered the art of exquisite food design, preparation and presentation. Qualified in French Patisserie, bakery, commercial cookery and hospitality management, he has worked in renowned five-star establishments, both in Australia and internationally.

Currently a hospitality, commercial cookery, patisserie and bakery trainer in Australia, Radley’s knowledge and flair for food design, can help to cater to any event or occasion.

Services

We offer a range of services to suit any occasion. From weddings, engagements to birthday parties, our elaborate cakes designs and catering services, we’ll make your event a memorable occasion. We also offer consulting services including restaurant consulting, menu & recipe design and workplace training.
